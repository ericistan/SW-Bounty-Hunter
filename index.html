Greetings, Bounty Hunter.

The galaxy is a dangerous place. As a bounty hunter, you are tasked with taking out the planet's most wanted criminals. But watch out for the green baby. The Mandalorian won't be happy if you mess with the kid.

How to Play:

  • Click characters as they appear in the holes to earn points
  • Stormtrooper — 10 pts  |  Dark Trooper — 50 pts  |  Hutt — +5 seconds
  • Avoid Grogu — clicking him costs you 1 health
  • Missing a shot or hitting Grogu resets your streak
  • You have 3 health and 60 seconds — good luck, hunter.
