----- SW BOUNTY HUNTER -----

The Bounty Guild has requested your help in clearing out the planet's most dangerous criminals. But watch out for the green child! The Mandalorians won't be happy if you mess with the kid.

How to Play

SW Bounty Hunter is a whack-a-mole style game. Shoot your way to top by racking up points and max streak before time runs out.

Click characters as they appear in the holes to earn points. Stormtroopers are worth 10 pts.

Dark Troopers are rare and fast, but worth 50 points. Don't miss them.

Target a Hutt to add +5 seconds to the clock. Keep an eye out.

Avoid the Child. Clicking him costs 1 health and resets your streak.
